Welcome to the Adventure!

The popular Nintendo Switch game New Super Mario Bros. U Deluxe combines two iconic platforming titles, "New Super Mario Bros. U" and "New Super Luigi U," into a single bundle. The game, developed by Nintendo, is a nostalgic yet modern spin on the popular Mario property.

Players engage on a side-scrolling adventure through a variety of colourful and imaginative environments, through a succession of difficult levels filled with obstacles, opponents, and hidden secrets. The game has both single-player and multiplayer modes, allowing friends and family to play together. New Super Mario Bros. U Deluxe, with its engaging gameplay, charming aesthetics, and famous characters, provides a pleasurable experience for both newbies and old fans of the Mario series.
