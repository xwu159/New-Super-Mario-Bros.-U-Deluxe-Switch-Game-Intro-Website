The History of the Game

"New Super Mario Bros. U Deluxe" is a platforming video game developed and published by Nintendo. Originally released for the Wii U in 2012, it followed the classic side-scrolling gameplay of the Mario series. An expansion called "New Super Luigi U" was later introduced.

In 2019, a deluxe version for the Nintendo Switch combined both games into one package. It received positive reviews for its enjoyable platforming, multiplayer modes, and nostalgic appeal. The Switch release widened its audience and solidified its place in the Mario franchise.

How to Play the Game

"New Super Mario Bros. U Deluxe" is a side-scrolling platform where players navigate vibrant worlds to rescue Princess Peach from Bowser. You control Mario, Luigi, Toad, or Toadette, each with unique traits. Jump, run, and defeat enemies using power-ups like Super Mushrooms and Fire Flowers. Explore hidden areas, face boss battles, and play cooperatively with up to four players. Collect coins for extra lives and reach checkpoints to progress. The game blends classic Mario gameplay with multiplayer fun, offering an exciting experience for players of all skill levels.

Tips and Strategies for Winning

  • Master the Basics: Practice your jumping and running skills to navigate through levels smoothly. Timing is crucial, especially when dealing with enemies and obstacles.
  • Utilize Power-Ups: Grab power-ups like Super Mushrooms and Fire Flowers to enhance your abilities. Fire Flowers allow you to defeat enemies from a distance, while Super Mushrooms provide an extra hit point.
  • Explore Thoroughly: Don't rush through levels. Take your time to explore every nook and cranny. Look for hidden blocks, secret paths, and collectibles like Star Coins.
  • Cooperate in Multiplayer: If playing in multiplayer mode, communicate with your teammates. Coordinate movements and share power-ups to maximize your progress.
  • Balance Speed and Caution: While it's tempting to rush, balancing speed with caution is important. Avoid running into unknown areas, as you might encounter unexpected hazards.
  • Patience is Key: Some levels require patience and precise timing. Don't be afraid to take your time to assess the situation before making a move.
  • Practice, Practice, Practice: The more you play, the better you'll become. Practice will help you improve your skills, learn level layouts, and become more confident in your play.
